The Cabinet Painting Process

1. Thorough Cleaning

We start by removing all grease, grime, and residue from your cabinets. Proper prep is everything‚Äîthis step ensures the paint adheres and lasts.

2. Sanding & Repair

Light sanding opens the surface for better adhesion. We fill dings, fix chips, and ensure a smooth foundation.

3. Priming

High-bond primer designed specifically for cabinets. This creates a sealed, uniform surface that prevents bleed-through and ensures durability.

4. Professional Painting

Multiple thin coats of premium cabinet paint applied with spray equipment for a smooth, factory-like finish. No brush marks, no drips.

5. Protective Topcoat

Catalyzed urethane or conversion varnish topcoat for maximum durability. This is the difference between paint that chips and paint that lasts years.

6. Reassembly

We reinstall doors, drawers, and hardware with care. Everything aligned, everything working smoothly. Your kitchen looks brand new.
